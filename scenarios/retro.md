PM | pm | Welcome to our sprint retrospective. This is a safe space, so please be candid. Let's start with what went well this sprint.
Dev Lead | dev | The deployment process was much smoother — the new CI/CD pipeline really paid off. We should celebrate that as a team.
PM | pm | Absolutely, well done everyone. Now, what could we improve? Be constructive — we're here to iterate, not to blame.
Dev Lead | dev | Honestly, communication between the front-end and back-end teams broke down mid-sprint. We need a better handoff process.
PM | pm | That's valuable feedback. I'll propose a daily async check-in between both teams. Can everyone commit to trialing this next sprint?
